23 cannolis: that’s what New York Congressman George Santos would have had to purchase from an Italian restaurant in Little Neck, NY, to reach his reported campaign spending total of $199.99 in an evening.

Nail technicians push for more workplace safety measures after ventilation regulations implemented

At a small salon off Jamaica Avenue in Queens, Maria Victoriano is both a nail technician and accidental chemist. While painting nails and assembling acrylic sets for 10 hours a day for the last 16 years, she interacts with hazardous chemicals the nail industry calls the “toxic trio:” toluene, formaldehyde, and dibutyl phthalate. Numerous studies have linked these chemicals to asthma, allergies, and pregnancy loss — all of which Victoriano says she has experienced.
